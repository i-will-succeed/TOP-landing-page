This Dog is Awesome!

Hello and welcome to the world of Reishi, also known as: "Bub". Just a reminder, this is Bub's world, we're all just living in it. He is a 1 1/2 year old Dachshund-Pomeranian Mix and is probably the most cutest creature you will ever encounter in this life.

He loves friends, cuddles, treats, sprinting around, performing circus-dog tricks, speaking his cute, squeaky language, always having something in his mouth, always wanting to play, and of course.. being a furry child of love.

All who meet him love him dearly, and I hope that you will fall in love with him also.
